# Knowledge is Power

A personal, educational static website dedicated to mental health awareness, recovery, healthy relationships, self-care, boundaries, addiction, trauma, integrity, and personal growth.

**Core belief**: Everyone should live with integrity — life would be much kinder, safer, and more honest if more people chose truth over manipulation. This site is built with that spirit: honest information to empower people to heal, grow, and build better lives.

## Purpose & Topics
This site provides reliable, compassionate information (not medical advice) on:
- Narcissism & narcissistic abuse
- Domestic violence & types of abuse
- Mental health conditions (ADHD, bipolar, depression, anxiety)
- Stress management, sleep hygiene, CBT techniques
- Addictions (substance use, gambling)
- Dual diagnosis (co-occurring mental health + substance issues)
- Trauma-informed care
- Healthy vs. toxic relationships & boundaries
- Integrity vs. duplicity
- Healthy living, self-esteem, self-care toolkits
- Quotes & affirmations for motivation

**Important Disclaimer**  
All content is educational only. It is **not** medical, psychological, or therapeutic advice. Always consult licensed professionals for personal concerns.  
In crisis? Call or text **988** (US Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or the National Domestic Violence Hotline at 1-800-799-7233.

## Technologies
- Pure HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript  
- Fully static — no backend, no frameworks, no database  
- Mobile-responsive (hamburger menu on small screens)  
- Interactive elements: self-care checklist (uses browser localStorage), basic quizzes
